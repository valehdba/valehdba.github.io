Sponsors & Partners

We are a volunteer-run, open-source community. Sponsorships help us organize meetups, workshops, and grow the PostgreSQL ecosystem in Azerbaijan.

Become Our First Sponsor

We're looking for organizations that believe in open-source technology and want to support the growing PostgreSQL community in Azerbaijan. Your sponsorship directly funds meetups, workshops, and community resources.

Contact Us About Sponsorship

Why Sponsor Us?

Brand Visibility

Your logo on our website, event materials, and social media — reaching PostgreSQL professionals across Azerbaijan.

Talent Access

Connect with skilled PostgreSQL developers, DBAs, and architects actively looking for opportunities.

Speaking Opportunities

Present your products, case studies, or technical talks at our meetups and workshops.

Community Impact

Support open-source education and technology adoption in Azerbaijan's growing tech ecosystem.

Sponsorship Tiers

Gold
  • Large logo on homepage
  • Logo on all event materials

  • Job board priority posting
  • Social media promotion

Silver
  • Medium logo on homepage

Bronze
  • Logo on sponsors page
  • Social media thank you
  • Event acknowledgment
