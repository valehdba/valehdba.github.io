Contribute to Local Community

Our community is built by its members. Whether you have 30 minutes or 30 hours a month, there's a way for you to help grow the PostgreSQL community in Azerbaijan. Tell us how you'd like to contribute!

We are a volunteer-driven community. Every contribution — big or small — makes a real difference. No prior organizing experience needed!

How You Can Help

Event Organizer

Help plan and run meetups, workshops, and conferences. Find venues, coordinate speakers, and manage logistics in Baku.

Speaker / Presenter

Share your PostgreSQL knowledge at meetups and workshops. Topics can range from beginner introductions to advanced deep-dives.

Content Writer

Write blog posts, tutorials, and guides in English or Azerbaijani. Help translate PostgreSQL documentation for local users.

Social Media & Outreach

Manage our Facebook, LinkedIn, Telegram, and YouTube channels. Promote events and share PostgreSQL news with the community.

Mentor / Tutor

Guide beginners through their PostgreSQL journey. Answer questions, review code, and provide one-on-one support to new members.

Website Developer

Help improve the postgresql.az website. Fix bugs, add features, improve design — the code is open source on GitHub.

Sponsorship Coordinator

Help find sponsors and partners for events. Build relationships with companies that use PostgreSQL in Azerbaijan.

Photographer / Videographer

Capture photos and videos at events for our gallery and YouTube channel. Help with livestreaming meetups and talks.
